Frequently Asked Questions

How do I copy a YouTube transcript?

Install Copy YouTube Transcript extension, open any YouTube video with captions, click the extension icon, and hit "Copy to Clipboard". The entire transcript is copied instantly.

Does this work with YouTube Shorts?

Yes! Copy YouTube Transcript works with both regular YouTube videos and Shorts, as long as they have captions available.

Can I include timestamps in the transcript?

Yes, you can customize the output to include or exclude timestamps, add spacing between lines, and even include the video title.

Is this extension free?

Yes, Copy YouTube Transcript is completely free with no ads or tracking. It's lightweight, fast, and respects your privacy.

Can I use the transcript with AI tools like ChatGPT?

Absolutely! There's even an optional AI summary prompt feature that formats the transcript perfectly for AI/LLM analysis.

What if a video doesn't have captions?

The extension requires videos to have captions/transcripts available on YouTube. Most videos have auto-generated captions, but some may not.

Does it work offline?

Once loaded, the extension works offline. However, you need internet access to load YouTube videos.
